# PRODUCT — ohmyself!

register: product

## What it is
A web app to view and converse with your "second brain": all your notes
(identity, goals, projects, people, journal, finances) stored as markdown, with
privacy levels. The web UI is the human window into the same brain that agents
reach over MCP/REST.

## Who uses it
You (the owner), authenticated, on desktop primarily. Calm, focused, often used
during planning/reflection. Future: a public read-only agent view for visitors.

## Core jobs
1. Sign up / sign in and get a brain seeded from a default template.
2. Browse notes by type/folder; read rendered markdown; see each note's privacy.
3. Search and filter quickly.
4. Ask the brain a question and get an answer grounded in your notes.

## Tone
Trustworthy, quiet, precise. This holds someone's whole life — it should feel
private and calm, never loud or gimmicky.
